Online registration service

Registration Note

This service allows parents to submit registration applications for their children and for all classes for the academic year 2020-2021, you must observe the following:

  • If you are the parent of a student who is currently registered in the operating schools of the smartschool system, and you have an account in the Parents and Students Gate, click on the "Enter as Parent" button to enter the Parent and Student Portal to submit a request to reserve a seat for the student or register a new student.
  • If you do not have an account in the Parents and Students Gate, you can create a new account by clicking on the "New Student" button to submit a request to reserve a seat for the student in another school or register a new student.
  • Online registration is available to students in all grades from kindergarten to twelfth grade.
  • Applicants must upload a copy of all required documents, for example: ID copy, birth certificate, proof of residence, etc.
  • All requests submitted will be verified through the registration section, and the applicant will be notified of any action taken on the application through the website through notification by text messages (SMS)
